Zulaiha D. Alhassan

Who is She?

Zulaiha is a social entrepreneur, Digital transformation executive, producer, and host. Her pool of experiences spans across various fields including digital transformation, producing, and hosting curated career and technology TV shows, professional career community building, leadership, training the youth in technology and more. As a digital transformation executive, she works to aid automate the manual processes and models of her organization to include and accommodate a more technological approach from coding software applications, training staff in Information Technology (IT) and developing tech policies to help scale the organization to meet the digital advancement of the world. Starting her social entrepreneurship journey in 2017, she is focused on inspiring and helping the youth especially women to grow and transform in their careers with focus in tech and media. She has over the years helped in bringing social impact and career development through organizing entrepreneurship and tech-related events when she was the co-director for Startup Grind Tamale, the grow with Google digital skills training program as a trainer and currently founder for Divaloper. She founded Divaloper in 2019 to inspire more females to consider venturing into careers in science, technology, engineering, arts, and mathematics (STEAM) and has recently added the Divaloper community of professional women wing to drive collaborations and innovations between women in diverse careers. She is an experienced speaker on various topics including women empowerment, digital transformation, future of work etc., She is currently the producer and a host of a TV show TechTV Ghana which is aimed at highlighting thrilling personalities, groundbreaking innovations and lasts tech reviews in the country. Zulaiha believes that each individual especially women should have access to tools and opportunities to help them in their careers and business to help make Ghana a better place ergo, she is committed to helping in that revolution using technology one community at a time. She holds a bachelor’s degree in Computing with accounting and is currently pursuing her master’s degree from University of Ghana in Computer Science.
